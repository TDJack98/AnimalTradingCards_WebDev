Bengal tiger

The Bengal tiger is also known as the Royal Bengal tiger and is the second biggest tiger among his species.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera tigris tigris
  • Average Length: 3 meters
  • Average Lifespan: 20 years
  • Habitat: Indian rainforest

Red-golden fur with black horizontal stripes and a white belly and white inside legs are the description of this species. With a length of nearly 3 meters and a weight of up to 258 kilos the males are as well as the smaller females a serious threat for their prey. Being to slow can be fatal for monkeys and peacocks.
